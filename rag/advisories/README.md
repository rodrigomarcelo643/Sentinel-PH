# Health Advisories

## Overview

Official health advisories, alerts, and community guidance from DOH and LGUs for distribution through the sentinel network.

## Purpose

Two-way communication: Not just collecting data, but also delivering health information back to communities through trusted local voices.

## Advisory Types

### 1. Disease Alerts
Warnings about active or emerging outbreaks

**Example**:
```
DENGUE ALERT - HIGH RISK
Cases increasing in Metro Manila. 
Eliminate standing water. 
Use mosquito repellent.
Seek medical care for persistent fever.
```

### 2. Prevention Reminders
Proactive health guidance

**Example**:
```
RAINY SEASON HEALTH TIPS
- Boil drinking water
- Wash hands frequently  
- Avoid wading in floods
- Check for mosquito breeding sites
```

### 3. Vaccination Campaigns
Information about immunization programs

**Example**:
```
FREE MEASLES VACCINATION
Health Center - June 15-20
For children 6 months to 5 years
Bring birth certificate
```

### 4. Health Service Announcements
Available health services and programs

**Example**:
```
FREE HEALTH CHECK-UP
Barangay Health Center
Every Monday 8AM-12PM
Blood pressure, blood sugar, weight
```

### 5. Emergency Advisories
Urgent health warnings

**Example**:
```
WATER CONTAMINATION ALERT
Do not drink tap water in Purok 3-5
Boil for 5 minutes before use
Free water distribution at barangay hall
```

## Distribution Channels

### Through Sentinels
- SMS to sentinel phones
- App push notifications
- Sentinel shares with community
- Word-of-mouth amplification

### Direct to Community
- Barangay announcements
- Church bulletins
- School notices
- Market posters

### Social Media
- Facebook community pages
- Viber groups
- Messenger broadcasts
- Twitter alerts

## Advisory Categories

### Seasonal Advisories

**Rainy Season (June-November)**:
- Dengue prevention
- Leptospirosis awareness
- Flood safety
- Water sanitation

**Summer (March-May)**:
- Heat stroke prevention
- Food safety
- Dehydration awareness
- Measles vaccination

**Holiday Season**:
- Food poisoning prevention
- Alcohol moderation
- Firecracker safety
- Travel health tips

### Disease-Specific

**Dengue**:
- 4S strategy (Search, Secure, Seek, Say)
- Warning signs
- When to hospitalize
- Prevention measures

**COVID-19**:
- Vaccination updates
- Booster schedules
- Isolation guidelines
- Testing locations

**Tuberculosis**:
- Symptom awareness
- Free testing
- Treatment adherence
- Contact tracing

**Diarrheal Diseases**:
- Handwashing importance
- ORS preparation
- When to seek care
- Water safety

## Emergency Protocols

### Immediate Alerts (Within 1 hour)
- Disease outbreak confirmed
- Water contamination
- Food poisoning cluster
- Environmental hazard

### Urgent Advisories (Within 24 hours)
- Rising case trends
- Seasonal disease warnings
- Vaccination campaigns
- Health service changes

### Routine Updates (Weekly/Monthly)
- General health tips
- Success stories
- Recognition announcements
- Training reminders

## Quality Assurance

### Review Process
1. Draft by health officer
2. Review by DOH liaison
3. Translation verification
4. Pilot test with sentinels
5. Approve for distribution

### Approval Authority
- **Barangay Level**: BHW Coordinator
- **Municipal Level**: Municipal Health Officer
- **Provincial Level**: Provincial Health Officer
- **National Alerts**: DOH Regional Office

## Archive & History

All advisories stored for:
- Historical reference
- Pattern analysis
- Effectiveness evaluation
- Training materials
- Research purposes

## Maintenance

- Review monthly for relevance
- Update with new guidelines
- Archive outdated advisories
- Translate to new languages
- Validate with health workers
